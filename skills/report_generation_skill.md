# Report Generation Skill

## Purpose

Turn validated source findings into a concise executive report for hydrogen stakeholders.

## Working Rules

- Lead with the business implication.
- Keep language direct and structured.
- Highlight what changed, why it matters, and what to watch next.
- Preserve source traceability in the final report.

## Suggested Report Sections

- Executive summary
- Key developments
- Market implications
- Technology signals
- Policy and funding signals
- Competitive notes
- Risks and watchouts

## Output Standard

- Clear headings
- Short paragraphs
- Action-oriented language
- Source list at the end
