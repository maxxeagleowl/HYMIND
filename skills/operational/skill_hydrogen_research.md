# Hydrogen Research Skill

## Purpose

Collect relevant hydrogen and fuel cell engineering, market, and funding intelligence
from external sources, scoped to Europe.

## Research Pillars

### 1. Funding — EU-wide, National, Regional
- EU Hydrogen Bank auctions and grants
- IPCEI Hydrogen project funding
- National programs: Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Netherlands, France
- Regional and state-level subsidies

### 2. Political Strategy
- EU hydrogen policy and regulatory updates
- National hydrogen strategy announcements
- EU Green Deal and REPowerEU hydrogen targets

### 3. Corporate Strategy
- Company hydrogen strategy announcements
- Partnership, JV, and offtake agreements
- Investment rounds and capacity expansions

### 4. Fuel Cell Systems / PEM Technology
- PEM fuel cell technology developments
- Manufacturer and competitor news (Europe-focused)
- Stack performance, cost, and durability advances
- System integrators and Tier 1 suppliers

### 5. Stationary Power
- Commercial fuel cell installations for stationary power
- Grid-connected and behind-the-meter applications
- Industrial and commercial energy supply

### 6. Micro-grid / Off-grid / Backup Power
- Hybrid micro-grid projects with fuel cell integration
- Off-grid and remote site power supply
- Backup and UPS applications (data centres, telecoms, critical infrastructure)
- Emergency and resilience power systems

### 7. Intralogistics
- Hydrogen fuel cell forklifts and warehouse vehicles
- Material handling fleet electrification
- AGV and autonomous intralogistics with fuel cell power
- European warehouse and logistics operator deployments

### 8. Green Hydrogen Supply Context
- Electrolyzer deployment in Europe
- Green hydrogen production costs and capacity

## Working Rules

- Prefer current, source-backed information (published within 90 days).
- Keep search terms tight and relevant to the pillars above.
- Capture source, date, and key takeaway for every item.
- Separate raw findings from interpretation.
- Geographic focus: Europe. Flag non-European news only if strategically relevant.
- Exclude: maritime/shipping, heavy road transport, passenger vehicles.

## Output Shape

- Topic / Pillar
- Source
- Date
- Summary
- Why it matters
- Confidence notes
