# Hydrogen Research Skill

## Purpose

Collect relevant hydrogen engineering and market intelligence from external sources.

## What To Look For

- Market developments
- Competitor updates
- Technology announcements
- Funding programs
- Policy changes
- Strategic alerts

## Working Rules

- Prefer current and source-backed information.
- Keep search terms tight and relevant.
- Capture the source, date, and key takeaway for every item.
- Separate raw findings from interpretation.

## Output Shape

- Topic
- Source
- Date
- Summary
- Why it matters
- Confidence notes
